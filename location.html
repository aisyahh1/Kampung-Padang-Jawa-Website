Kampung Padang Jawa (Petaling) is a traditional village located between 2 cities namely Klang City and Shah Alam City. The distance from the city of Klang is about 8 Km and from Shah Alam about 5 Km. This village is very unique because it is under the administration of 2 districts, one part under the administration of Klang District and the other part under the administration of Petaling District. The boundary line between these two districts is located in the middle of this village.
