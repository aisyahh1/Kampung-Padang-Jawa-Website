Studio Gunting

Introducing Studio Gunting, a well-known barbershop where professionalism meets precision grooming in the heart of Kampung Padang Jawa, Petaling. Their skilled barbers are dedicated to providing their clients with an elevated grooming experience, ensuring they step out looking polished and confident. Studio Gunting offers flexible grooming hours that fits with the schedule of their clients. Whether it's a midday touch-up or an evening transformation, Studio Gunting will be there for you.

The shop is open from Monday to Saturday and services start from 3:30 p.m. until 11:30 p.m.. The store is closed every Sunday so it is best to visit the shop on a weekday to experience an amazing haircut!

Restoran Mak Aji Bistro

Welcome to Restoran Mak Aji Bistro, where culinary excellence and cultural flavors unite to create an unforgettable dining experience! Often visited by people who lives near the restaurant, from office workers to college students. Restaurant Mak Aji offer their customers with more than 100 traditional malay side dishes to choose from and believe it or not the side dishes can be obtained at a reasonable price.

Restaurant Mak Aji Bistro is well-known for their signature side dish, the Ikan Patin Masak Tempoyak. This culinary masterpiece combines the delicate taste of freshwater fish (Ikan Patin) with the rich, tangy notes of Tempoyak, a traditional fermented durian paste. It is a harmonious blend of flavors that promises a journey through the authentic tastes of Malaysian cuisine that makes visitors would want to go for another round of meal! Plus, the restaurant also provides an outdoor seating for customers who prefer to eat outside the area.
