Things To Do in Kampung Padang Jawa

Annual Sports

Every year, Kampung Padang Java organises engaging sporting events or activities in which everyone is welcome to join. There are activities suitable for all ages.

Firefly

You can go see the fireflies in the Klang River which is a breeding ground for Fireflies which are a type of bird that glows at night. You can take beautiful pictures at that place
