The picture on the left is the head of Kampung Padang Jawa, Petaling Tuan Haji Hisham Bin Parman (on the left side). The role of a village head is to be responsible for conveying to the villagers all the policies of the government and advising them to obey and be loyal to the ruling government in addition to providing projections, encouragement, and resistance such as striving toward progress and development of themselves, families and the village, practicing the instructions religion and adhere to national customs that are appropriate to the needs of the times, encourage the villagers to pay any revenue to the government as prescribed by law, provide reasonable assistance to government employees who carry out their duties in the village, encourage the formation of moral unity and cooperation with each other and prevent and stay away from bad actions.

The picture above is the Majlis Pembangunan Komuniti Kampung (MPKK) chairman of Kampung Padang Jawa, Petaling Tuan Abd Halim Bin Hassan. The role of the MPKK chairman is that they are individuals appointed by the state government for a period that has been set as facilitators and liaisons between the government and the local community and act as intermediaries at the grassroots level who will channel any problems or complaints to relevant parties.
